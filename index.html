The Pipeline

From idea to post
in minutes.

Every step of content creation is automated. The only human involvement is approving the result.

01
🗓️
Topic Queue
n8n pulls a topic on schedule — twice daily with randomized jitter.
02
✍️
Script Generation
Claude writes a 60-90 second script tuned to the channel's voice and audience.
03
🎙️
Voice Synthesis
ElevenLabs converts the script to a cloned voiceover with perfect pacing.
04
🎬
Video Generation
Higgsfield AI generates cinematic 9:16 clips matched to each visual prompt.
05
🎵
Final Assembly
FFmpeg assembles clips, burns subtitles, mixes ambient music, renders the final MP4.
06
📲
Review & Post
Discord notification with the video. Approve 🔥, and it posts automatically to TikTok.
